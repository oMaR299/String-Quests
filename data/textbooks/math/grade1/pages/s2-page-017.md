| | | | | | | |
|---|---|---|---|---|---|---|
| Ones | tens | add | plus | sum | equals | subtract |
| take away | difference | | count on | count back | | |

### Add

6 + 6 = 12 &emsp; 10 + 2 = 12 &emsp; 4 + 8 = 12 &emsp; 7 + 5 = 12

### Subtract

12 - 6 = 6 &emsp; 12 - 2 = 10 &emsp; 12 - 8 = 4 &emsp; 12 - 5 = 7

- To find the sum of
